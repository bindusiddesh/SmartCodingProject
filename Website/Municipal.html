Introduction

Stockholm municipality or informal Stockholm city is a municipality in Stockholm County . Central map is Stockholm, which is also Sweden's capital and residence city for Stockholm County.
The municipality is situated in the southern parts of the Uppland and northern parts of Södermanland . To the east lies the Baltic Sea with the Stockholm archipelago and in the west Mälaren . In the southeast, the municipality borders on Tyresö municipality , in the south to Huddinge municipality and Botkyrka municipality , to the west to Ekerö municipality , in the north to Järfälla municipality , Sollentuna municipality and Danderyd municipality , in the northeast and northwest to Sundbyberg municipality and Solna municipality , in the east to Lidingö municipality. municipalityand Nacka municipality , all in Stockholm County.
